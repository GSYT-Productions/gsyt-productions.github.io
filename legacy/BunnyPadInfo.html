What is BunnyPad?


BunnyPad is an innovative alternative of Windows' Notepad/WordPad dedicated to GarryStraitYT's Best friend, PBbunnypower (Bunny). Development is ongoing, and the app currently is on version 6, which is known as EggHunt.

Why did you switch from NSIS to Inno Setup after version 1?

Well, upon running the NSIS Installer in VirusTotal, we discovered that NSIS modified our program to "phone" an IP address we did not recognize. We at GSYT Productions saw this as a breach of our terms, and thus discontinued NSIS from being used for our products, and switched to Inno as it does not phone any IP addresses, is more customizeable, and has less of a detection rate in VirusTotal.
