What is BunnyPad?
BunnyPad is a note-taking app developed by GSYT Productions.
How many downloads does BunnyPad have?
As of the last checking, BunnyPad has a total of 186 downloads.
Who contributed to the development of BunnyPad?
According to itch.io, BunnyPad has been contributed to by jpbandroid, ZeRoTeCh00, PBbunnypower, GarryStraitYT, Ducky, Come On Linux, BunnyFndr, and Bluejay.
Who is BunnyPad dedicated to?

Who created BunnyPad?
BunnyPad was created by Garry Strait, the founder of GSYT Productions.
What is BunnyPad made in?
BunnyPad is made in Python, using the PyQt5 library as the GUI.
What is some lore behind the BunnyPad icon?
BunnyPad's icon does have lore, but not even GarryStraitYT (Garry Strait) knows what's canon or lore involving the icon, as it was PBbunnypower who created the icon.
What is PrettyFonts?
PrettyFonts is the proprietary engine behind BunnyPad. PrettyFonts is designed to fit fluently against Windows 11.
